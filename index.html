Beware of publicly scanned QR Codes 

If you've scanned a QR code to get here, it's important to learn about QR code safety. QR codes can be convenient, but they can also link to malicious sources. Here are some tips to stay safe:

  • Do not scan QR codes found in public places unless you trust the source.
  • Always verify the URL before clicking any links.
  • Use a QR code scanner that provides a preview of the URL.
  • If you have gone to a QR code link, avoid giving any personal information until you can verify the source is legitimate.

We all make mistakes, and it's okay. Moving forward, remember to think twice before engaging with links and QR codes. Stay safe!
